About

Undergraduate Software Engineer Hassan Mahmoud

A third year student at Faculty of Engineering, Alexandria University, majored in Computer Engineering & have found my passion at Web Design & Web Development.

I was born & raised in Alexandria, Egypt, I was always mature for my age. during my lifetime I always invest in myself. I did well in school. People often call me self-contradictory who loves consistency but at the same time loves to do unexpected & new things which seems to be weird at first but then they find out that those things are worth to give a try. I wasn't that good at knowing my personality, manage it & know how to deal with the surroundings. Unfortunately, self-awareness wasnâ€™t something I learned until many years later.

In high school, I began to tire of the pressure placed on me by my family and their high expectations. I gradually stopped my extra-curricular activities, including design & photo-editing, and chose to work instead. I graduated at the top of my class, In college, I was involved in few outside activities. I participated in Microsoft Student Partner tech club & that was a turning point in my life as it was where I've found my passion for web design & development because I've found a way to use my designing skills in something I love, related to my college study & from which I can make a living.

The year 2016 is the best year of my life. determination and love for what I do has landed me in a pretty interesting place with some neat people. In this I received my very first job as a junior web developer at a software house based in my country. I build custom websites, and provides design solutions for a wide-array of clients at this company, Savvy Arabia .
